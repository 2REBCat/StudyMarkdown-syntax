# Alert
`> [!NOTE]` is
> [!NOTE]
> It is useful info

`> [!TIP]` is
> [!TIP]
> Helpful advice

`> [!IMPORTANT]` is
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Key info

`> [!WARNING]` is
> [!WARNING]
> Urgent info

`> [!CAUTION]` is
> [!CAUTION]
> Advises about risks
